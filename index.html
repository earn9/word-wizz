word wiz!

how to play

  1. Each round, our Wizard will give you a key word.
  2. You have 20 seconds to think of related words.
  3. Input your word in the Wiz Out! section and hit ENTER. (The Wizard only understands correct spelling!)
  4. If your word matches our Wizard's word list, you get a point!

Accumulate as many points as you can to out-wiz our Wizard!

hint!

Think outside the box! For example, words for 'Reptile' could include: extinct, scaly, gecko, tiny, tropical, dinosaur
